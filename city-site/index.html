🎯 Abu Dhabi Era Highlights

  • → Sheikh Mansour's takeover in September 2008 transformed City into a global powerhouse
  • → First Premier League title in 44 years in 2012 — sealed in the final second by Sergio Agüero
  • → Pep Guardiola appointed in 2016, beginning the most dominant period in English football history
  • → First — and only — Treble in English football's Premier League era, 2022–23
  • → Four consecutive Premier League titles (2021–22 to 2024–25) — an English record
