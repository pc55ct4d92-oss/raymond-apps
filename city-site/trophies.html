Historic Achievements

Records & Landmarks

⭐ The Treble — 2022–23

Premier League, FA Cup, and Champions League in the same season. The first English club to win the continental Treble since Manchester United in 1999. Widely considered the greatest achievement in English club football.

⭐ Domestic Treble — 2018–19

Premier League, FA Cup, and Carabao Cup — the first ever English Domestic Treble. The 98-point PL season was the third-highest ever. The FA Cup final saw Watford beaten 6–0 in one of the most dominant final performances in history.

📊 100 Points — 2017–18

The Premier League record. 32 wins from 38 games. 106 goals. 19-point winning margin. City became the first team in English football history to surpass 100 points in a single top-flight season.

📊 4 Consecutive Titles — 2021–24

An unprecedented four consecutive Premier League titles from 2020–21 to 2023–24, the first men's team in English top-flight history to do so. A record that may never be surpassed.

🏆 FA Cup Final Classic — 1956

Perhaps the most famous cup final in history. Goalkeeper Bert Trautmann — a German ex-prisoner of war — played the final 17 minutes with a broken neck, making crucial saves. City won 3–1. The courage was extraordinary.

⚡ AGÜERO! — May 13, 2012

Sergio Agüero's 93rd-minute winner against QPR secured the Premier League title by goal difference — ending 44 years of hurt. Voted the greatest Premier League moment in multiple polls. The commentator's famous words echo through history.
